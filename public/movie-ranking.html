The Best Movies According to Marceau

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

TRON

An Sci Fi movie where the main character gets digitized into a computer world of programs. The movie was far ahead of its time not in only in concept but also film techniques. The soundtrack is provided by Wendy Carlos which gives the computer world an extra dimension.

5 Seconds per Centimeter

A very well drawn and immersive anime, where the story is divided in 3 parts. It tells about young love and the deep yearning and feeling of loss when the 2 main characters are separated and never see each other again. The director and writer is Makoto Shinka, who's eye for perfection lifts this anime to greater hights. Music by Tenmon.

Departures

A story of a Japanese cellist whose orchestra gets disbanded and together with is wife moves to his home town looking for work. He starts a job as nokanshi, which is a funeral professional who prepares the deceased bodies before burial. Its a story about life, death, humor and closure. Music by the famous Joe Hisaishi.
